One time we found an old pig pen and decided we’d clean it up and make it our fort. After spending hours cutting the grass with scissors, we figured we needed paint to snaz it up. Since we didn’t have any money, we thought it would be a good idea to pick berries and make pigment out of that. Once we realized how many berries it would be, we completely forgot about the place.
